Sticky Scrollytelling

Watch the visualization change as you scroll through the story

 
 

Introduction

Start with a simple visualization. In this case, we begin with a single circle. As you scroll, watch how it transforms.

 

Step One: Expansion

New elements appear around the center. Each circle represents a different data point or concept in your story.

 

Step Two: Growth

The visualization evolves. Notice how the circles grow larger, emphasizing their importance in the narrative.

 

Step Three: Distribution

Elements spread out, showing relationships and connections. This is where your data story becomes clear.

 

Conclusion

The final state of your visualization. All elements come together to tell the complete story.
